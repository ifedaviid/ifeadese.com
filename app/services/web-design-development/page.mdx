## Web Design & Development

I make websites for individuals and small businesses. Websites that are neat, fast, mobile-responsive and user-friendly. Here's a typical workflow:

- **Initial Chat**: ~20 mins. We'll talk about your project, timeline etc.
- **Research & Design**: Plan according to our discussion and seek your feedback.
- **Build**: Proceed to build what we've agreed on.
- **Handover**: Necessary deliverables given to you.
- **Launch**: Make it live and share.
