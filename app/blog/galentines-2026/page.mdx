# Galentines 2026: The Love of God Conference

Galentines Global is an annual Christian women's conference held every February in Ottawa. For the 2026 edition—themed "The Love of God"—the organizers needed a site that could handle registration, recruit volunteers, showcase speakers, and tell the story of a growing movement.

## What Was Delivered

A warm, celebration-centered design built with Next.js and deployed on Vercel:

- **Two-step RSVP system** via Formspree with confirmation emails and spam folder tips
- **Live countdown** updating in real-time—days, hours, minutes, seconds
- **Speaker showcase** with responsive grid and mobile carousel
- **Team profiles** featuring the founder and leads for Operations, Communications, and Programming
- **Volunteer recruitment** with dedicated form
- **Contact form** for questions, feedback, and testimonies
- **Live stream integration** linking directly to YouTube for remote attendees
- **SEO structured data** (Schema.org Event markup) for search visibility

The visual language—gradient logo, image carousels, scripture verse in the hero, generous spacing—reflects a feminine, faith-centered brand. Typography and layout feel inviting before attendees even arrive.

## Results

The site launched smoothly as the central hub for conference communications. Updates happened quickly. The RSVP flow captured registrations without friction.
