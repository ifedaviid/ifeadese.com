# Galentines 2026: The Love of God Conference

Galentines Global is an annual Christian women's conference held every February in Ottawa. For the 2026 edition, themed "The Love of God", I built them a site that could serve as the central point for communications and interactions with their audience. This meant a site that handle their event registrations, recruit volunteers, convince partners, showcase speakers, and just tell the story of a growing movement.

## What Was Delivered

A gradient logo with a warm, celebration-centered website theme showcasing photos from previous editions of the event. The objective was simply to reflect a feminine, faith-centered brand. Built with Next.js and deployed on Vercel.

- **7 pages** in total
- **Dedicated forms** for general contact, RSVPs, feedback and volunteer recruitment
- **Data storage & Email Confirmations** via Formspree integration
- **Partnership page** to help communicate and create support opportunities
- **Team page** featuring profiles of its founder and team leads
- **Custom branding** with gradient logo, rose-coral palette, and Parkinsans text font
- **Fast performance** via static generation and Vercel edge deployment
- **SEO optimization** for search visibility

This site helped funnel through up to 200 RSVPs, 15+ form submissions for new feedback, volunteers, testimonials etc.
